If you're looking for a new sport to play, or are interested in coming along and giving it a go, just join us at one of our twice-weekly training sessions and we'll show you the ropes!

We're open to all ages, abilities, and genders, students and non-students alike. Trainings every Wednesday from 2-5pm and Saturday 12-3pm, on the Meadows, by middle meadow walk (on the map to the right).
